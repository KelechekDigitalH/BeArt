OUR COMMITMENT

BeART is at the forefront of creating disruption in the status quo with its unique and trustworthy platform. The platform brings the best talent in the field within a single ecosystem. We bring to the digital world exclusive physical paintings and pieces of jewelry via creating insured hybrid NFTs. These NFTs can ultimately enable art lovers to own valuable assets.

Strategic Vision

BeART aims to be the most trusted destination for art of owning an exclusive synergy of physical & digital assets.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enrich the world of paintings & jewelry via digitalization.

BeART addresses the most important issues in the era of digitalization

  • Not insured NFTs
  • NFTs copyrights
  • Losing interest in traditional art assets
  • Higher attention to the development of digital assets
  • Involvement of jewelers and artists in the NFT market

WE BELIEVE

Physical art assets should be valued and preserved as a legacy for future generations. We believe that modern technologies will benefit the real world by facilitating access to valuable assets.
